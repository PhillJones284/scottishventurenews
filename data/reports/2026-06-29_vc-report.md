## 3. Deal Spotlight

### Aveni — Growth — £12m
**Lead investor**: PXN Ventures · **Co-investors**: Puma Growth Partners, Lloyds Banking Group, Nationwide, Scottish Enterprise · **Sector**: AI & Fintech · **Location**: Edinburgh

University of Edinburgh spinout Aveni builds AI-powered compliance tools for wealth managers and retail banks, with its Unified Assurance Platform analysing financial advice interactions at scale. The £12m will fund two new products — Agent Assure and Agent Approve — designed to assess the conduct risk of AI agents making decisions in financial services workflows. Lloyds Banking Group and Nationwide joined the round both as investors and, as Aveni's primary target market, as prospective customers of the tools it's building.

Source: [Edinburgh Innovations](https://edinburgh-innovations.ed.ac.uk/news/fintech-startup-aveni-announces-12m-raise)

### Go Swag — Seed — $5m (~£3.7m)
**Lead investor**: Mercia Asset Management · **Co-investors**: Techstart Ventures · **Sector**: SaaS / Corporate Gifting · **Location**: Glasgow

Glasgow-based Go Swag operates a tech platform for branded merchandise and corporate gifting, counting Meta, Apple, Netflix, and ElevenLabs among more than 1,000 corporate clients. The $5m seed round, led by Mercia with Techstart Ventures participating, brings total funding to $6.2m.

Source: [Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com/discover/funding_rounds/e3120c30307a4df552a2e1c7559da4a2)
