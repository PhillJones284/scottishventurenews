## This Week

## The Numbers

Q2 2026 stands at **19 deals worth £107.5m**; year-to-date that's **29 deals worth £141.6m**. Both figures are unchanged from last week's issue.

**Most active investors this quarter, by deal count**: Tricapital leads with 4 deals (£2.76m), ahead of Scottish Enterprise Investment Fund with 3 deals (£22.5m), and a tie for third between Maven Capital Partners and STAC Invest, both on 2 deals (£3.85m and £0.52m respectively).

**By capital deployed**: Index Ventures and Highland Europe are tied at the top, each putting in £51.9m across a single deal, followed by Scottish Enterprise Investment Fund's £22.5m spread across three deals.

Seed remains the most common stage this quarter, with Deep Tech and Life Sciences the two sectors drawing the most repeat capital; Edinburgh continues to account for the largest share of deals, with Glasgow and the rest of Scotland roughly level behind it.
