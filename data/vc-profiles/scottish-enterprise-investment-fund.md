**Trajectory**: Structurally constant and the most frequently appearing investor name in the ledger. A thirteenth historical deal has been added since the last update — Gigged.AI's £1m seed round — but it carries no confirmed announcement date, so it falls outside both the trailing-6mo and YTD windows: 12 of the 13 deals fall within the trailing six months (0 prior), and twelve Scottish deals are counted so far in 2026, totalling £48.05m across confirmed amounts. SEIF co-invests alongside private capital across virtually every stage and sector and does not lead rounds or set terms — its presence signals that a lead investor has already committed, not that public capital is driving the deal. For founders, SEIF involvement is close to a given for any credible deal involving Scottish angel syndicates or institutional co-investors. No independent thesis can be read from its deal selection given its breadth.
