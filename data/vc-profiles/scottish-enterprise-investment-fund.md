### Scottish Enterprise Investment Fund
**Historical deals in ledger**: 11 (Aveni, Lentitek, Exergy3, Earth Blox, Biocaptiva, TileBio, Quantcore, Launchpad, Nami Surgical, Carbogenics, Gigged.AI)
**Sectors favoured**: AI & Machine Learning, Healthtech, SaaS & Enterprise Software, Energy & Cleantech, Fintech, Deep Tech, Life Sciences, Climate tech / Nature risk analytics
**Stages**: Seed, Series A, Growth
**HQ**: Glasgow
**Trajectory**: Structurally constant and the most frequently appearing name in the ledger, with 10 of its 11 deals falling in the trailing six months. SEIF co-invests alongside private capital across virtually every stage and sector rather than leading rounds or setting terms — its presence signals that a lead investor has already committed, not that public capital is driving the deal. For founders, SEIF involvement is close to a given for any credible deal involving Scottish angel syndicates or institutional co-investors. No independent thesis can be read from its deal selection given its breadth.
