3. Deal Spotlight

Aveni — Growth — £12m

Lead investor: PXN Ventures · Co-investors: Puma Growth Partners, Lloyds Banking Group, Nationwide, Scottish Enterprise Investment Fund · Sector: AI & Machine Learning, Fintech · Location: Edinburgh

University of Edinburgh spinout Aveni builds AI compliance tools for banks and wealth managers. Its Unified Assurance Platform monitors financial advice quality and conduct risk in real time; newer products — Agent Assure and Agent Approve — are designed to assess the behaviour of AI agents operating in client-facing financial services. The £12m round was led by PXN Ventures, with Puma Growth Partners returning and Lloyds Banking Group and Nationwide joining as strategic investors. Scottish Enterprise also participated. Aveni plans to deploy the capital on its agent-focused product lines.

Source: Edinburgh Innovations, scottishfinancialreview.com
